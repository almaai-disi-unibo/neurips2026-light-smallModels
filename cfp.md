# Call for Papers

LIGHT invites submissions on compact, trustworthy and deployable AI systems.

## Topics of interest

- Distillation of foundation-model capabilities into compact models
- Small language models and compact multimodal systems
- Trustworthiness-by-design through model compression
- Neuro-symbolic extensions for compact foundation models
- Explainability, reasoning and verification in small models
- Robustness and safety of compressed AI systems
- Runtime guarantees for deployable AI
- Efficient adaptation beyond fine-tuning
- Edge and resource-constrained deployment
- Sustainable AI through compact architectures
- Benchmarks and evaluation methodologies
- Industrial deployment of compact and trustworthy AI

## Submission types

We welcome:

- extended abstracts
- position papers
- system demonstrations
- industrial deployment reports
- reproducibility contributions
- benchmark contributions

## Review process

All submissions will be handled through OpenReview and will receive at least three reviews from the Program Committee.

## Important notes

The workshop will not accept work already published at the NeurIPS main conference or other venues, as stated in the workshop policy.
