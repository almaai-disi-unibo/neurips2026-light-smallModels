# Organizers

## Program chairs

- **Roberta Calegari**, University of Bologna — Primary contact
- Dennis Hoppe, HLRS
- Joachim Koehler, Fraunhofer IAIS
- Michela Milano, University of Bologna

## Short bios

**Roberta Calegari** is Professor of Artificial Intelligence at the University of Bologna and works on trustworthy AI, fairness, neuro-symbolic AI and explainable AI.

**Michela Milano** is Professor of Artificial Intelligence at the University of Bologna and Director of the Digital Society Center at Fondazione Bruno Kessler (FBK). Her research spans trustworthy AI, decision support systems and hybrid reasoning-learning-optimization approaches.

**Dennis Hoppe** is Head of Converged Computing at HLRS and Project Manager of HammerHAI, focusing on scalable AI infrastructures and industrial AI adoption.

**Joachim Koehler** is Head of the NetMedia Department at Fraunhofer IAIS. His work includes AI systems, machine learning and deployment of trustworthy AI systems in real-world environments.
